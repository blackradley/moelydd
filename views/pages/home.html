In these pages there are four toolkits; their purpose of is to provide prompts to clarify thinking about what and who your service is for, as well as a project or projects you are (or intend on) initiating.

The questions are specifically designed to lead your project planning in enterprising and strategic ways.

By answering the questions in these toolkits, you will be able to better define:

  • What your project is for;
  • Who it is for;
  • What problems you may encounter, and;
  • A starting position from which to proceed.

The four toolkits can be used individually for numerous projects or collectively to define various perspectives of a single project.

The input you provide in these pages can be exported in to a word document to use as a reference point once your project is initiated.
