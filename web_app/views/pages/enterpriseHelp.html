Click on each bar (i.e. Intent, Themes etc.) and put in the relevant information. Each bar will be unlocked when the previous bar has been completed.

You can view your progress in a document at any time by clocking the 'View Document' link.

Intent

On this screen the user is asked to define the intent of the enterprise activity. The intent is broken down into three elements (1) socio-economic priority, (2) impact and timeframe and artistic/cultural positioning.

Socio-economic priority: E.g. "We want to raise aspirations and increase employment."

Impact and timeframe: E.g. "Reducing inequality to UK average levels and doubling the size of the creative sector by the end of the decade."

Artistic/Cultural Positioning: E.g. "By turning our joint and diverse heritage into an innovation engine for the local economy"

Commercial

On this screen you can add what opportunities there may be for your enterprise activity E.g. Opportunity 1: "Sell Outreach sessions to schools."

Opportunity 2: "Sell consultancy on how to deliver profitable outreach to other schools."

Opportunity 3: "Loan developed heritage materials to other museums for their outreach."

Resources

On this screen you can add what resources you have, or will need, to begin your enterprise activity

E.g. Assets: "Utilise the developed heritage materials to create the loan packages."

Staff: "Marketing team may be able to design sales prospectus for the package."

Information: "A similar project failed at another museum because……."

Support: "Counsellors want greater emphasis on heritage in classroom. A counsellor could be used for a public launch."

Priorities

On this page you can describe what the priorities are for your enterprise activity from three key angles; operational, financial and political.

E.g. Operational: "Improve our profile amongst minority ethnic audiences."

Financial: "Must deliver 20% profit above investment in the first year."

Political: "Position this county as leading the way in tackling low aspirations in schools and enterprise in museums."

Action

On this screen you can begin to pull the project together and set out a pathway to get started. I.e.:

Vision: what is the key sales line to make people enthused with doing this?

1st Steps: what tasks will you do first to start it off?

Discomfort: how do you make whoever is comfortable with not doing it uncomfortable with the status quo?
