Intent

On this screen the user is asked to define the intent of the philanthropic and fundraising activity. The intent is broken down into three elements (1) socio-economic priority, (2) impact and timeframe and artistic/cultural positioning.

Socio-economic priority: E.g. "We want to raise aspirations and increase employment."

Impact and timeframe: E.g. "Reducing inequality to UK average levels and doubling the size of the creative sector by the end of the decade."

Artistic/Cultural Positioning: E.g. "By turning our joint and diverse heritage into an innovation engine for the local economy."

Priorities

On this page you can describe what the priorities are for your exhibition or tour from three key angles; operational, financial and political.

E.g. Operational: "Achieve political support for our wider programme."

Financial: "Must deliver 20% profit above investment in the first year."

Political: "Position this county as encouraging disaffected groups to engage with politics."
